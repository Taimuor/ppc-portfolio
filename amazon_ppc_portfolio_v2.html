Real Challenges Faced
PPC Struggles & How We Tackle Them
📈
ACOS Rises When Scaling
April proved it — pushing spend up 46.8% MoM to capture more volume pushed ACOS from 21.70% to 24.29%. Scaling into new placements and audiences always introduces inefficiency. The key is monitoring ACOS ceiling thresholds vs total profit impact.
🎯
Keyword Intent Mismatch
March had 526 purchases vs 742 in February despite similar budgets. Broad match terms pull in informational queries from non-buyers. Tight phrase and exact match control, combined with weekly search term mining, is essential to maintain purchase rates.
🏷️
Negative Keyword Gaps
The Feb data showed an SP Auto campaign running for "Bamboo cutting board" — an automotive brand bleeding into unrelated categories. Without robust negatives at campaign and ad group level, spend leaks into irrelevant searches at scale.
💰
Budget Allocation Pressure
Top-of-search % varied: 10.85% in March vs 18.79% in April for the main campaign. Budgets capping mid-day means losing premium placements when purchase intent peaks (typically 8–11pm). Dayparting and budget scheduling are critical fixes.
🛒
Listing CVR Ceiling
With 2,657 detail page views in a single week vs proportionally fewer purchases, there's a conversion gap at the listing level. Even perfect bids fail when images, bullets, or A+ content underperform. Listing quality is the hidden multiplier for PPC efficiency.
🌍
Multi-Country Complexity
Running US + CA simultaneously means separate auction dynamics, currency differences (CA$28,469 visible in recent data), different customer behavior patterns, and split attribution reporting. Each market needs its own bid strategy and budget segmentation.
